[Question]
Can I add multiple people at once to a static list?

[Answer]
Yes, you can add multiple people to a static list by uploading a spreadsheet or using the copy and paste feature directly inside the list.
---

[Question]
Can I add someone to a list who is not already in the system?

[Answer]
No. Only existing subscribers can be added to a list. If someone is not yet in the system, they must be added as a subscriber first.
---

[Question]
Can I combine two static lists into one?

[Answer]
Yes. Go to "custom list manager" and use the "combine or split other lists" option to merge two static lists into one.
